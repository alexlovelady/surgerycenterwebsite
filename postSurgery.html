Post Operation and Pain Relief

Your pain management is a top priority. We often use a variety of methods to ensure your comfort, including oral medications, intravenous medications, nerve blocks, and local anesthetic injections during surgery. Before the procedure, it’s important to discuss your pain management plan with both your anesthesiologist and surgeon. Don’t hesitate to voice any concerns or fears you may have. Understanding pain management will help set proper expectations, leading to a smoother and more comfortable recovery.
