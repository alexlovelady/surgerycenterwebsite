Check-in and Admission

Check-in:
When you arrive at our center we ask that you check-in at the registration desk to verify personal, escort and insurance information.

Following Admission:
After checking in at our center, you will be brought to the pre-op area where the nurse will do a health interview and prepare you for surgery. An anesthesiologist will discuss your anesthesia with you and answer any questions. At the appropriate time you will be escorted to the operating room. During surgery your escort will stay in a waiting area and will rejoin you in the recovery area after your surgery.
