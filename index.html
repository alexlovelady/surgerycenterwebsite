Your Health, Our Priority

At Sage Surgical Center, we offer cutting-edge surgical care with a commitment to excellence. Our experienced team of surgeons is here to guide you every step of the way, from consultation to recovery.

Before Surgery

We aim to make your surgery day as smooth as possible, so you can arrive, check in at the front desk, and be guided to your room without the hassle of completing extensive paperwork.

Our registration process is quick and simple, taking about 20-25 minutes. Be sure to have your insurance details ready when filling out the form.

The Day of Surgery

Upon arrival at our center, please check in at the front desk to confirm your personal, companion, and insurance details.

Our Day of Surgery Guide will provide you with all the necessary information on how to prepare for your procedure.



After Surgery

Our top priority is providing clear and easy-to-understand recovery instructions for you and your family. For more details, please refer to our guide.

Ensure you have arranged transportation for a safe return home after your procedure. Consult our After-Surgery Guide for additional important information.

Our Specialties

Laparoscopic Cholecystectomy

A minimally invasive procedure to remove the gallbladder, commonly performed to treat gallstones.

Bariatric Surgery

A set of surgical procedures performed to help patients with obesity achieve significant weight loss and improve health.

Chronic Pain Treatments

Treatment options for chronic pain including medications, physical therapy, nerve blocks, and minimally invasive procedures to help manage and reduce pain.

Fecal Incontinence Solutions

Medical and surgical treatments designed to improve bowel control and reduce incontinence episodes.

Urinary Incontinence Solutions

Surgical procedures like sling procedures and artificial sphincters to restore control over urinary function.
