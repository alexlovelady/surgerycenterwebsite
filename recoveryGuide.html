Recovery and Reminders

You will be given both verbal and written instructions for post-surgical care, and it is important to follow them closely. Your escort will need to review and sign these instructions before you are cleared to go home. If you notice anything out of the ordinary after your surgery, contact your surgeon or visit the nearest emergency room. A nurse from our center will follow up with you the day after your surgery to check on your recovery.
