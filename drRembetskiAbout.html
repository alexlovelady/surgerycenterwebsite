Meet Our Surgeons

Dr. Rembetski

General Surgeon

Dr. Thomas Rembetski is a board-certified surgeon specializing in general and laparoscopic surgery with a passion for providing high-quality care to his patients. With over 40 years of experience, Dr. Rembetski has developed expertise in a wide range of surgical procedures, including minimally invasive techniques, complex abdominal surgeries, and emergency surgical care. After earning his medical degree from Boston University School of Medicine, Dr. Rembetski completed his residency at University of Colorado Medical Center and Oregon Health Sciences University, where he honed his skills in both general and specialized surgical practices. He is committed to staying at the forefront of surgical advancements, regularly participating in continuing education and training programs. Throughout his career, Dr. Rembetski has been recognized for his meticulous surgical technique and compassionate approach to patient care. He is a member of several professional organizations, including Washoe County Medical Society and Nevada State Medical Society, and has contributed to numerous publications and presentations within the field of surgery. At Sage Surgical, Dr. Rembetski is dedicated to delivering personalized care, ensuring that each patient receives the most effective and least invasive treatment possible. His commitment to excellence is evident in his patient outcomes and the trust he has earned from both his patients and colleagues. In addition to his clinical practice, Dr. Rembetski is actively involved in mentoring the next generation of surgeons, sharing his knowledge and experience to help shape the future of the field.
